Recipe of the Day:Kabuli Palaw

Kabuli palaw,or Qabuli Palaw, is Afghanistan's national dish, a flavorful rice dish featuring tender meat(often lamb or beef),
aromatic spices, and a sweet-savory mix of caramelized carrots and raisins.
